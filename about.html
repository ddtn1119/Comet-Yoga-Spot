About Us

Our Story

Comet Yoga Spot was founded in 2020 with the vision of creating a peaceful and welcoming space for individuals to explore the art of yoga, mindfulness, and well-being. Our studio is built on the principles of inclusivity, self-discovery, and personal growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people of all backgrounds and skill levels to experience the benefits of yoga. We believe in fostering a strong mind-body connection, promoting relaxation, and cultivating a sense of inner balance.

Meet Our Instructors

Emily J.

Emily is a certified yoga instructor with over 10 years of experience specializing in Hatha and Restorative yoga. She is passionate about helping students find balance and tranquility through yoga practice.

Michael L.

Michael brings energy and strength to every class. With a focus on Vinyasa yoga, he enjoys guiding students through dynamic flows while emphasizing breathwork and alignment.

Mana A.

Mana specializes in meditation and mindfulness-based yoga practices. She believes yoga is a journey of self-discovery and encourages students to explore their inner potential.

Our Studio

Located at a walking distance from the University of Texas at Dallas at Richardson, TX, our studio is designed to be a sanctuary where you can escape daily stress. We provide a warm and inviting environment with mats, blocks, and blankets for all attendees. Enjoy our Serenity Lounge before and after class!
